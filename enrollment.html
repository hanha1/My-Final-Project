Enrollment

The SBCBS Online School offers 3 Levels of Enrollment

      1. Auditing Level:Take courses in any order.
    • Free online video Bible lessons.
    • Free syllabus for each course.
    • No required assignments.
    • No certificates awarded.

      2. Informal Level: Take courses in any order.
    • Free online video Bible lessons.
    • Free syllabus for each course.
    • Free course notes (viewed online).
    • Free testing.
    • Free graded assignments.
    • Free course certificates.

      3. Formal Level: Take courses in designated course schedule order.
    • Free online video Bible lessons.
    • Free syllabus for each course.
    • Free course notes (viewed online).
    • Free testing.
    • Free graded assignments.
    • Free course certificates.
    • Free diploma upon successful completion of all courses.
